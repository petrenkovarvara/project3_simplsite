ABOUT

The Habsburgs ruled one of the most powerful empires in European history. Originating from Austria, their dynasty expanded its influence across the continent — from Spain to Hungary — through diplomacy, marriage, and conquest.

For nearly six centuries, the Habsburgs shaped the course of politics, religion, and art. Their courts became centers of learning, music, and cultural brilliance.
